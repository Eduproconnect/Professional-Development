Activity Handout: Monday Morning Plan

Purpose

To create a concrete implementation plan for relationship-building strategies that you can begin using immediately in your classroom.

Time Required

30 minutes

Materials Needed

  • This handout
  • Pen or pencil

Instructions

Part 1: Strategy Selection (10 minutes)

  1. Review all the relationship-building strategies discussed today.
  2. Select one strategy you will implement immediately (tomorrow or Monday).
  3. Complete the detailed planning section below.

Part 2: Pair-Share (10 minutes)

  1. Partner with a colleague to share your implementation plan.
  2. Provide feedback and suggestions to each other.
  3. Refine your plan based on feedback.

Part 3: Small Group Refinement (10 minutes)

  1. In groups of 4, each person will briefly share their plan.
  2. The group will offer specific suggestions for:
  3. Potential challenges and solutions
  4. Ways to enhance the strategy
  5. How to assess effectiveness

Reflection Questions

  • What excites you about implementing this strategy?
  • What concerns do you have about implementation?
  • How might this strategy impact your classroom culture?
  • What will success look like for this implementation?

Remember

The most effective relationship-building happens through consistent, small actions over time. Don't try to change everything at once. Start with one manageable strategy, implement it well, assess its impact, and then build from there.
